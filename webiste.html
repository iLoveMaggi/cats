What is a cat?

The cat (Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae and is often referred to as the domesticated cat .

Why get a cat?

Getting a cat can sound daunting at first. Be it that you saw that YouTube video of cats being jerk or worrying that you dont have time to take care of it.

I am here to assure you that having a cat is a breeze compared to other pets:

  • Cats are actually one of the few independent domesticated pets!
  • They don't require as much attention/affection as dogs do!
  • Whats the first thing you think of when they say "Litter box?", it's cat!

Cat breeds

Now that some doubts have been taken off your shoulders, it is time I introduce you to some cat breeds! They can range to cute, tiny to huge cats!

  1. Maine Coon. This is my personal favourite! It one of the largest domesticated cats in the world and is well known for its playfulness and friendly nature, hence its nickname, gentle giant.
  2. Birman. Similarly, this cat is easy-going and affectionate, great for households with multiple pets and children.
  3. Persian. This cat has a distinct facial feature similar to bulldogs. Great for bulldog lovers!
  4. Abbysinian. AKA Abys, is a short haired cat with a distinctive coat. They are more active and encourage play like canine does.
